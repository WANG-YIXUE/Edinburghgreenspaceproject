 What do the map layers show?

    The ARQI - Edinburgh layer displays our index in each Datazone in Edinburgh. You can hover over the datazones to find out their score.
    The Greenspaces - Edinburgh layer displays the greenspaces included in our study. You can hover over the greenspaces to find out their score.
    The SIMD layer displays the Scottish Index of Multiple Deprivation. You can hover over the datazones to find out their score.
    The Greenspace Access Points layer shows the access points of each greenspace.
    The Heatmap layer shows where the greenspace access points are clustered in Edinburgh.

With the measure tool you can measure distances and areas such as the distance between a location and a greenspace access point or the area of a greenspace.
For visual help, please use the User Guide in the navigation bar on the top of the page.
